WATER BACKUP

Backup water storage and pressure systems for supply interruptions

Wedrill Water designs backup water systems that keep properties supplied during municipal outages, low-pressure periods or high-demand usage. Systems can integrate boreholes, tanks, pumps and filtration.

  • Storage tank sizing for household, estate, business or farm demand
  • Booster pump and pressure-control installation
  • Borehole and municipal supply integration
  • Optional filtration before water reaches taps or appliances
